No app store needed. No technical knowledge required. Just follow these steps — it takes about 2 minutes.
⭐ EASIEST — USE A LAPTOP
Use a Computer + Netlify Drop (Free)
1
Save the app file to your computerDownload the foodguard.html file to your Desktop or Downloads folder.
2
Open Netlify Drop on your computerOpen any browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari) and go to:

app.netlify.com/drop

You will see a big grey area that says "Drag and drop".
3
Drag the file onto the pageFind the foodguard.html file on your computer and drag it onto the Netlify Drop page. Drop it in the grey area. Wait about 10 seconds.

✅ It's free. No account needed. No sign-up.
4
Copy the link Netlify gives youAfter uploading, Netlify shows you a link like:
shiny-cookie-abc123.netlify.app

Copy that link or email it to yourself.
5
Open the link on your phoneOn your phone, open the link in:
— Chrome (Android / iPhone)
— Safari (iPhone)

The camera and torch will work perfectly.
6
Add it to your Home Screen On Android (Chrome): Tap the 3-dot menu ⋮ at the top right → tap "Add to Home screen" → tap Add.

On iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share button ⬆ at the bottom → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add.

✅ FoodGuard is now on your phone exactly like a real app — tap it anytime to open!
📱 NO COMPUTER NEEDED
Phone Only — Use Manual Mode
1
Send the file to yourselfEmail or WhatsApp the foodguard.html file to your own number or email.
2
Open the file in your browserTap the attachment in your email or WhatsApp. When asked how to open it, choose Chrome or Safari — not a document viewer.
3
Use Manual Input modeIn this mode, the camera may not work (phones require a special link for camera security). Tap "Type Ingredients Manually" and paste the ingredients from the food label. Everything else works perfectly offline.
❓ Why does the camera need a web link?
For your safety, phones only allow camera access from secure web addresses (starting with https://). When you use Netlify Drop, your file gets a secure link automatically — completely free, no account needed, ready in under 1 minute.
